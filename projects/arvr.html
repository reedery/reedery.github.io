Augmented and Virtual Reality

Humans are social beings– we form communities and share stories. And as we have gone from cuneiform script to oil painting to IMAX cinema, our species has always employed the latest in technology to push the limits of what kinds of stories we can share.

Whether realistic, or artistically abstracted in any way, most modern forms of media consist of a blend of various, often older, artforms (engineering among them). Brad Bird, director of The Incredibles and Iron Giant (my favorite film of all-time), says he loves all of the arts, but "movies most because they combine so many of them." I wholeheartedly agree with this, and it is by extension of his logic that makes me extremely excited about a future with stories being told in augmented and virtual reality.
