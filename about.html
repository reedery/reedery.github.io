About Me

I studied Computer Science and was a distance swimmer at Boston College. Now, I love training for and competing in triathlons, reading sci-fi and solving problems with technology. I'm also an Ocean Lifeguard for the LA County Fire Dept. during the Summer months.

I'm guided by my curiosity and so far that's lead me to be a part of some really special projects and learn about some incredible things. I'm fascinated by Virtual Reality and Data Science as I believe these will shape the human condition more so than anything else.

I'm currently a Software Engineer at The VR Company, building technology to tell meaningful stories.
