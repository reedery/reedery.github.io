I am a Software Engineer with experience building applications in Virtual Reality and Data Science/Machine Learning.  I am a recent graduate of the Computer Science Dept. at Boston College and am passionate about the intersection of art, sociology and technology.  Away from the computer, I'm an avid fimmaker,  competitive triathlete and Ocean Lifeguard for the Los Angeles County Fire Department.

JoyceStick (2016)

Bringing James Joyce's timeless classic Ulysses into Virtual Reality with Unity (C#) for the HTC Vive and soon to Google Daydream.

K-Nearest Images CV/LS-HASH (2016)

Using Locality-Sensitive Hashing and computer vision to  efficiently  retrive the k-nearest images in a dataset to a given image.

Boston College in Virtual Reality  (2016)

Using a 3D-printed 360 stereoscopic camera rig to shoot panoramas of BC's campus. Experience was then built in Unreal Engine.

Procedural Text Generation (2016)

Using a Recurrent Neural Network built in Python with Keras and Theano to generate text based on 600 Simpsons episodes and comparing resutls to a Markov Model.

Motion Graphics & Timelaspe (2015)

Various video related small project created in Adobe After Effects, Illustrator and Premiere

Smartphone Data-stream Processing (2015)

Queing a live datatream from an Android Application with Kafka for data-viz in JS.

Making it Irish VR (2014)

Built a 360 VR experieince to accompany Making it Irish, a McMullen Museum exhibition celebrating the Irish Arts/Crafts Movement.

Mod of Cards (2014)

Six-episode (30- minutes each) web series reimagining Netflix's House of Cards taking place at Boston College.

Yelp Dataset Analysis (2013)

Implementing a Bayesian filter in R to find the best restaurants in Austin, TX.
